Mental model when reading an abstract

  1. Topic ‚Üí the writer provides background factual information.

  2. Problem ‚Üí The writer presents the gap/problem to treat

  3. Research Gap ‚Üí the writer states the limits of current knowledge.

  4. The purpose : the writer presents the general aim and the specific aim of the study.

  5. Methodology ‚Üí the writer summarises the methodology and provides details.

  6. Results ‚Üí the writer indicates the achievement of the study.

  7. Perspectives ‚Üí the writer presents the implications of the study.
